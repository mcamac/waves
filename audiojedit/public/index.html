AudioJedit

Welcome to AudioJedit!

Use the search box to search sounds on SoundCloud and load them by clicking the avatar. Once loaded you can extract selections of the waveform into a new one.

  • Use your mouse to make selection of the waveform. Press Esc to cancel.
  • Press Enter to add that selection to a new waveform.
  • Press Space to play the newly composed sound.
  • Press S to stop.
  • Press R to reset it if you don't like it.
  • Press U to upload to SoundCloud.
